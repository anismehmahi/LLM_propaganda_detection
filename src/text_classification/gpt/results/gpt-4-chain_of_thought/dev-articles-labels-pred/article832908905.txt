Loaded_Language - The use of the term "witch hunt" by Trump to describe the Mueller investigation carries a strong emotional impact and implies that the investigation is baseless and politically motivated.

Repetition - The article repeats the statement that there have been no indictments related to alleged Trump campaign collusion with Russia, emphasizing the lack of evidence found for collusion.

Doubt - The quote from Harvard law professor Alan Dershowitz, stating that the report is “a prosecutorial document presenting only one side” and urging the public “to withhold final judgment until they hear the other side of the story,” introduces doubt about the credibility and completeness of the Mueller report.
