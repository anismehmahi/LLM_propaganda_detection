Exaggeration,Minimisation - The article exaggerates the extent of censorship and the power of the elite, while minimizing the potential impact of social media platforms and mainstream media.

Causal_Oversimplification - The article assumes that the censorship of independent media is solely due to the actions of the elite, without considering other factors such as the spread of misinformation or the need for regulation.
